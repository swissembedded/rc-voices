# Voice target

The whole pack speaks in one consistent voice. Target profile for
selecting the Bark speaker preset:

| Attribute | Target | Reading |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Fundamental F0 | ~ 163 Hz | low, warm female register |
| F0 spread | narrow | steady, calm - little melody |
| Delivery | dry, studio | announcer, not conversational |
| Phrase length | ~ 1 s | short callouts |

Audition step: start from a female preset (e.g. `v2/en_speaker_9`),
compare neighbours on 3 phrases, keep the one whose pitch and steadiness
sit closest to the table. We match a voice *profile*, not any specific
person.
